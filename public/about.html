Droid Tycoon RB Planner

About

Droid Tycoon RB Planner is an independent, fan-made tool built to help Droid Tycoon players plan their next rebirth: see what credits and droids you'll need, and check which droids are safe to sell, worth keeping, or worth upgrading before you rebirth.

It's built and maintained by an independent fan of the game, not by the game's developer or publisher. Droid Tycoon, its droids, and all related names and assets are the property of their respective owners.

Where the data comes from

Rebirth requirements are community-sourced and cross-checked against player-maintained guides and spreadsheets. The game updates over time, so requirements can shift — see the RB Requirements table for the full data set, and always verify at the in-game Rebirth Station before selling anything.

Why there are ads

This site is free to use and supported by ads, which keeps it running without needing accounts or payments. See the Privacy Policy for details.
