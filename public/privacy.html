Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026

Droid Tycoon RB Planner ("this site") is an unofficial, fan-made tool for players of Droid Tycoon. This page explains what little data is involved in using it.

Information we collect

We don't have accounts, sign-ups, or a server-side database. The only data involved is stored locally in your own browser (via localStorage): your rebirth level, selected cycle, theme preference, and ad-visibility preference. This information stays on your device and is never sent to us.

You can review or opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings, and learn more at Google's Advertising Policy.

Hosting

This site is hosted on Cloudflare, which may automatically log standard technical information (such as IP address, browser type, and request timestamps) for security and performance purposes, per Cloudflare's own privacy practices.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
